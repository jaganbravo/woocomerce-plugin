# Dataviz AI WooCommerce - Competitor Pricing Snapshot

Last updated: 2026-04-30

This is a public-pricing snapshot of products that overlap with Dataviz AI for WooCommerce (AI analytics, reporting, profit analytics, attribution, and eCommerce intelligence).

## Competitor list and pricing

| Competitor | Positioning vs Dataviz AI | Public pricing (starting point) | Notes |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Metorik | WooCommerce-focused analytics + reports + lifecycle emails | Starts at **$25/month** (order-volume based) | 30-day free trial; pricing scales by monthly orders. |
| Putler | Multi-source eCommerce analytics and customer insights | Starts at **$20/month** (revenue-based) | Metered billing by monthly revenue slabs; 14-day trial. |
| Metrilo | eCommerce analytics + CRM + retention/email | Starts at **$199/month** (Essential) | Higher-end pricing tiers; 14-day trial. |
| BeProfit | Profit analytics and business reporting for eCommerce stores | Starts at **$49/month** | WooCommerce + other platform support; multi-tier plans. |
| Conversios (All-in-One Pixel Pro) | Tracking/attribution + pixels + feed sync | Starts around **$89.1/year** (promo pricing shown) | WooCommerce-focused tracking suite; annual plans shown. |
| MonsterInsights (with WooCommerce tracking) | WordPress GA reporting and WooCommerce tracking | Starts around **$99.50/year** (intro pricing shown) | Renewal shown at higher standard price; not a dedicated AI copilot. |

- Pricing changes frequently (promotions, annual vs monthly billing, volume-based or usage-based tiers).

- Some competitors are not WooCommerce-only, but are included because buyers evaluate them for similar analytics/profit workflows.
